Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients: 200g Spaghetti 100g Ground Beef 1 Onion, chopped 2 Garlic cloves, minced 400g Tomato sauce Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions: Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions. In a pan, sauté the onion and garlic until translucent. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.

Chicken Salad

Ingredients: 200g Grilled chicken breast, sliced Mixed salad greens Cherry tomatoes, halved Cucumber, sliced 1 Avocado, sliced Salad dressing of choice

Instructions: Arrange the salad greens on a plate. Top with grilled chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado. Drizzle with your favorite salad dressing. Serve immediately.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients:

    1 Banana 100g Strawberries 1 cup Milk or yogurt 1 tablespoon Honey (optional) Ice cubes

Instructions:

    Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: 500g Chicken breast, cubed 1 cup Yogurt 2 tablespoons Butter 1 Onion, finely chopped 2 Garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon Ginger paste 1 cup Tomato puree 1/2 cup Cream 2 tablespoons Garam masala Salt to taste

Instructions: Marinate the chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and garam masala for at least 2 hours. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's no longer pink. Pour in tomato puree, cook for 10 minutes. Stir in cream, simmer for 5 more minutes. Serve with naan or rice.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients: 250g Paneer, cubed 1 cup Yogurt 1 tablespoon Ginger-garlic paste 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder 1 teaspoon Red chili powder 1 teaspoon Garam masala 1 tablespoon Lemon juice Salt to taste 1 Onion, sliced 1 Bell pepper, sliced

Instructions: Marinate paneer cubes in yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for 1 hour. Thread paneer, onion, and bell pepper onto skewers. Grill or bake until golden brown. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

    2 cups Dosa batter 2 Potatoes, boiled and mashed 1 Onion, finely chopped 1 Green chili, chopped 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder Curry leaves Salt to taste Oil for cooking

Instructions:

    Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and onions. Add green chili, turmeric, and mashed potatoes, cook for 5 minutes. Heat a tawa, spread dosa batter thinly. Place the potato filling inside the dosa, fold and serve with chutney.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients: 500g Chicken, cut into pieces 2 cups Basmati rice 1 Onion, sliced 2 Tomatoes, chopped 2 Garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon Ginger paste 1 cup Yogurt 2 tablespoons Biryani masala 1 teaspoon Saffron soaked in milk 4 cups Water Salt to taste

Instructions: Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger, garlic, and biryani masala for 1 hour. Fry onions until golden, add tomatoes and chicken, cook until chicken is done. Boil rice with salt and water until 70% cooked. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, drizzle saffron milk, cover and cook on low heat. Serve with raita.

Chhole Bhature

Ingredients:

    1 cup Chickpeas, soaked overnight 2 Onions, chopped 2 Tomatoes, chopped 2 Garlic cloves, minced 1 tablespoon Ginger paste 2 tablespoons Chole masala 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder 1 teaspoon Red chili powder Salt to taste Oil for cooking 2 cups All-purpose flour (for Bhature) 1/2 cup Yogurt (for Bhature)

Instructions:

    Cook chickpeas until soft, drain and set aside. Heat oil, sauté onions, garlic, ginger until golden. Add tomatoes, cook until soft, then add spices and chickpeas. Simmer for 10 minutes. For Bhature, knead flour with yogurt and water, rest for 1 hour, then roll out and deep fry. Serve hot with chole.

Aalu Paratha

Ingredients:

    4 Potatoes, boiled and mashed 2 cups Whole wheat flour 1 Onion, finely chopped 2 Green chilies, chopped 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds 1 teaspoon Red chili powder 1 tablespoon Coriander leaves, chopped Salt to taste Ghee or oil for cooking

Instructions:

    Mix mashed potatoes with onions, green chilies, cumin, red chili powder, and coriander. Knead the dough with flour, water, and salt, and let it rest. Roll out a small dough ball, stuff with potato mixture, and seal. Roll out the stuffed dough, cook on a hot tawa with ghee until golden brown. Serve with yogurt or pickle.
